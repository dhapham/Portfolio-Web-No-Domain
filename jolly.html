Logo Design

Jolly Days Bakery

Meet Jolly Days, a cozy little bakery in Toronto, lovingly run by a mom with two kids. She’s passionate about cakes and pastries, and she wanted her brand to reflect that same passion and joy.


My creative work

When it came to designing the logo, she had a clear vision. She wanted something that looked hand-drawn and cute, but also elegant. Yellow was a must-have color because it symbolizes happiness—exactly what she wants her customers to feel when they enjoy her cakes.

The result? A logo that’s as warm and inviting as her delicious treats, perfectly capturing the heart and soul of Jolly Days.
