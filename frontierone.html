Branding Package & Marketing

Frontier One Inc.

Frontier One Inc. is a fingerprinting agency accredited and authorized by the RCMP to electronically capture and submit fingerprints. Frontier One is one of the largest fingerprinting agencies across Canada, with more than 14 offices.


Our team work

As a designer and part of the manager team at Frontier One Inc., I have been responsible for creating designs for our brands over the past three years.
For this branding package, the biggest task was redesigning the website, transitioning from Wix to WordPress using UX Builder to avoid hard coding. I also made minor HTML and CSS adjustments as needed.

Our goal was to create a more professional, government-related design, reflecting our growth to over 14 locations across Ontario and Alberta.

In this position, I have enhanced my skills in multimedia design, including graphic design, video editing and production, as well as web development and marketing. I am confident that I am contributing to the success of this company.
